08 · Longevity

Care & maintenance

  1. Dust all surfaces regularly with a soft damp cloth, in the grain direction. Do not use spray polishes or those containing silicone.
  2. Lift objects when moving them; never drag across a finished surface. Keep sharp metal objects — keys, spring binders, briefcase hinges — from contacting the finish.
  3. Place wet objects on coasters; use a desk pad for writing.
  4. Protect the bottoms of items such as flower vases and desk accessories.
  5. Contact with certain plastics may cause discoloration and finish damage.
  6. Use felt pads under electronic equipment.
  7. Furnishings exposed to ultraviolet rays may change color.
  8. Lubricate drawer-suspension housings and ball bearings every 6 months with a high-quality petroleum-jelly-type lubricant.
  9. Clean pull hardware with a window-type cleaner sprayed onto the cloth, not the hardware.
  10. Furniture settles over time — check that units are level and adjust glides to keep drawers aligned.
