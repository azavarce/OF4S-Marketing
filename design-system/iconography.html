04 · Foundation

Icons that get out of the way.

A linear icon set built on a 24px grid with rounded joins. Drawn at 1.5px stroke for clarity at small sizes. Same family across web, decks, proposals, and process diagrams — never mix and match.

4.1

The drawing system

Every icon is built on a 24×24 grid with a 2px outer keyline. Strokes round at every junction. Counters are open. Shapes geometric, never decorative.

  • 01

    24×24 grid, 2px keyline

    All icons fit a 20×20 visual area inside a 24×24 box, leaving 2px breathing room on every side.

  • 02

    1.5px stroke at native size

    Stroke scales with size: 1px at 16, 1.5px at 20–24, 2px at 32+.

  • 03

    Round caps and joins

    Rounded line endings keep the family feeling humanist — not hard or industrial.

  • 04

    Open counters, no fills

    Strokes describe shape; we never fill except for indicator dots and selected states.

  • 05

    Geometric, not illustrative

    If an idea needs a literal drawing, use photography. Icons are signs, not pictures.
