7.2

Composition rules

Apply across every slide for a consistent rhythm.

RULE 01

120px outer margin

Generous whitespace separates us from busy catalog decks.

RULE 02

One headline per slide

Display type carries the emotional weight. Body type fills detail when needed.

RULE 03

Mono eyebrows always

Every slide opens with a Geist Mono eyebrow at +0.22em tracking.

RULE 04

Italic = emotion

Reserve italic Fraunces for the line that should land. Don't italicize whole headlines.
