Five principles that make every decision easier.

P / 01

Editorial over corporate.

Confident typography and considered whitespace beat clip-art and stock corporate gradients. Always.

P / 02

Show real work.

Photographs of completed installs out-perform any rendering or stock shot. If we don't have the photo yet, mark a placeholder — don't fake it.

P / 03

Navy is a frame, not a fill.

Use deep navy for moments of weight — covers, callouts, footers — not as a default page background. Paper-toned neutrals are the canvas.

P / 04

The "4" is the mark.

Our most recognizable letterform. It earns the role of monogram, favicon, social avatar, and visual punctuation.

P / 05

Less surface, more system.

Reuse the same components everywhere — proposal tables, deck slides, web cards. The system compounds the more we use it.
