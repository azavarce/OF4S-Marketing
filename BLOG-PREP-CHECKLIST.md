# Next Project Blog — Prep Checklist

Keep this open while you gather material for the next "Explore Our Projects" post.
The more of this that's ready **before** we start, the faster the draft comes together.
Two parts: **(A) photos to drop in the Drive folder**, and **(B) facts to have ready to paste.**

---

## A. Photos — drop these in ONE Google Drive folder, then send me the folder link

Make **one folder per project.** Inside it, try to cover every slot below. If a slot has no
good photo, that's fine — tell me and I'll leave it out gracefully.

| # | Slot | What it is | How many |
|---|------|-----------|----------|
| 0 | **Cover / banner** | The single strongest **LANDSCAPE** hero shot (finished space or on-site). Used as BOTH the blog-list thumbnail and the wide banner at the top. Must crop well wide, subject roughly centered — avoid portrait/awkward crops. | 1 |
| 1 | **Client logo** | Clean client logo, transparent background if possible (PNG/SVG). **Put the word "logo" in the filename** (e.g. `umusic-logo.png`) — that keeps it from becoming click-to-zoom. | 1 |
| 2 | **Salesperson / designer at work** | A real photo of the OF4S person **visibly working on THIS project** — at the CET/laptop screen, or walking the space with the client. **Not** a finished empty room. This is the first photo readers see. | 1–2 |
| 3 | **2D floor plan** (optional) | The plan screenshot/export, if you have one. | 0–1 |
| 4 | **3D renders** | Renders of the key spaces. **Flag any render that has a matching installed photo of the SAME area** — those two become the signature "render vs. finished" pair. | a few |
| 5 | **Furnished-space photos** | Finished shots of each space: open workstations, private offices, executive offices, lounge, collaborative area, conference rooms, etc. | several |
| 6 | **Installation photos** | In-progress install shots: staging/boxes on the floor, assembly, team on-site. | 2–4 |
| 7 | **Final-result photos** | Your best finished shots (some can double as furnished-space photos). | several |

**Partner logos are NOT needed in the Drive** — the AIS / Corp Design / Global / Narbutas etc.
grayscale logos already live in Odoo and I pull them automatically. Only add a manufacturer's
logo if it's an unusual partner not in our standard set (and say so).

### Photo prep tips that make it fast + safe
- **Web-size them.** Export at roughly **1600–1920 px on the long edge, ideally under ~300 KB each.** Full-size phone/camera originals are heavy and slower/riskier to handle.
- **Name files by slot** so mapping is instant, e.g. `cover-lobby.jpg`, `salesperson-cet.jpg`,
  `render-lounge.jpg`, `installed-lounge.jpg`, `final-conference.jpg`, `install-staging.jpg`.
- **"logo" in logo filenames** (client and any partner logos) so they don't become click-to-zoom.
- For a **render ↔ finished pair**, name them so the match is obvious (`render-lounge` / `installed-lounge`).

## B. Facts — have these ready to paste (short bullet notes are perfect)

**Client**
- Client name
- Type / sector (e.g. "Hospitality & Entertainment", "Law firm")
- Location (City, ST)

**Scope**
- Spaces furnished (comma list)
- Furniture partners / manufacturers used (e.g. AIS, Corp Design, Source International)

**Project stats — real numbers only**
- Total square feet
- Weeks, order → install
- Number of workstations
- Any other real number worth showing

**Story notes — short bullets, NOT full sentences (I write the copy in our voice)**
- Discovery — what they needed, their goals/culture
- Design & layout planning — how the plan came together (CET real-time, feedback)
- What we helped furnish — the spaces + any shared palette/finish theme
- The installation — phased? on schedule? minimal disruption?
- The final results — the outcome / how it feels

**Media**
- 3D project video — **Vimeo ID, numbers only** (e.g. `1202238113`) — or "no video"
- Which photo is the **cover** (or ask me to propose one)

**Extras (optional)**
- A real client or team quote **+ who said it**
- Salesperson / designer name (for the at-work photo caption)
- Post title — or ask me to propose 2–3
- SEO title / description — or ask me to propose

## The fast path once it's all ready
1. Send me the **Drive folder link** + paste the **facts** above.
2. I map each photo to its slot and show you the mapping to confirm.
3. I draft the copy in OF4S voice and assemble the post as an **unpublished draft** in Odoo.
4. You review, upload the photos into the named slots (or I upload web-sized ones), and click **Publish**.

*Nothing goes public until you publish it.*
